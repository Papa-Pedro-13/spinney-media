About Us

About Spinney Media

We at Spinney Media are building an easy, affordable, subscription-based physical distribution and fulfillment service for all working and aspiring musicians.

Join our mailing list! It's not just a way to stay in touch. We'll be asking a select number of our mailing list subscribers to join our beta launch and follow on pilots as we work toward launching our platform for everyone.

About Rob, Founder-CEO

Rob Bach has over thirty years experience working in music retail and distribution, including key operations and leadership roles with Virgin Megastores, Amazon, and CD Baby.

His professional and personal networks within the industry are vast and ready to help independent musicians connect and thrive.

About Rob, The Early Years

  • Played violin and piano through grade school
  • Noodled on his Yamaha DX7 synthesizer during high school
  • Gigged as a mobile DJ for three years
  • Finished his BA in English/Creative Writing at the University of Washington in 2001
